From sometime now I have been noticing that I cannot actually set up my priorities correctly. Thus focus is missing from my execution. I try to juggle too many balls at the same time and end up getting all of them on the floor!

Some key soft skills one must be conversant with to be successful in this world are:
- Time management
- Networking
- Data and Information Organization
- Delegation (Possibly to the computer!)

Each of these deserves a blog of their own but I shall try to elaborate on my thoughts on these topics in later blogs.
